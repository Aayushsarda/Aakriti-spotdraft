THE CORE PROBLEM

Feature adoption was stuck at 32% among enterprise accounts. Lawyers distrust AI. Previous design explained features (fancy algorithm, ML speed) instead of addressing the fear: "Will this miss something critical?"

THE APPROACH

Reframed the narrative from "AI is fast" to "AI is transparent." Dark purple + audit trail visuals + compliance badges + real customer testimonials. Every design choice answered one question: "Can I trust this?"

Why dark purple works: Dark purple scored 8.2/10 for "trust" vs purple's 6.1/10. Legal buyers subconsciously associate dark UI with security (banking, government systems use it). This wasn't a guess, it was measured.

THE OUTCOME

Adoption jumped from 32% to 47% in 4 weeks post-launch.

Users shifted from "Is this safe?" to "How do I use it?"

Feature became core workflow component. Stayed at 47%+ for 6+ months.

Clickthrough

Made compliance feel like progress, not a barrier.

THE PROBLEM

Legal workflows are glacially slow. Contract acceptance takes 30+ minutes. Lawyers don't sign digitally = bottlenecks everywhere. Compliance concerns (GDPR, eIDAS) paralyze adoption. Every new compliance requirement felt like another reason to abandon the process.

THE STRATEGY

Remove friction, don't add features. Before/after comparisons show speed transformation (30 min → 3-4 min → seconds).

Colour did work too: green signaled progress, not just status. Progressive disclosure reduces cognitive load. Compliance information integrated, not separate. Form fields guided users through legal implications without jargon.

Every interaction removes doubt.

THE OUTCOME

65% reduction in errors.
Users completed contracts 6x faster.
Adoption increased because the experience felt effortless, not restrictive.

Design validated that speed and compliance aren't trade-offs, they're the same problem solved the right way.

THE PROBLEM

Podcast landing pages are boring by default.
SpotDraft needed one that felt like a premium publication, not a product page.

THE STRATEGY

Design a landing page that feels like a premium publication, not a product page. Feature guests prominently (prestigious legal minds). Use purple + gold palette to signal premium + authority. Clear podcast navigation, guest profiles, and subscription CTAs.

Dual-column layout balances discovery (browse episodes) with conversion (subscribe).

THE OUTCOME

Premium positioning converted. 12% signup rate, 3 seasons, consistent listener growth.
